# The European Union and North Korea: Time for a Strategic Reset?

Policy brief examining the European Union’s approach toward North Korea and evaluating the effectiveness of sanctions, diplomacy, and selective engagement strategies.

## Project Overview
This paper analyses the EU’s current position toward North Korea within the broader context of:
- nuclear proliferation,
- regional security,
- humanitarian concerns,
- and geopolitical competition involving China, Russia, the United States, South Korea, and Japan.

The brief evaluates whether continued isolation and sanctions remain effective and explores alternative approaches based on limited technical dialogue and humanitarian engagement.

## Key Topics
- EU–North Korea relations
- Nuclear deterrence and sanctions
- Humanitarian diplomacy
- China and Russia’s role in North Korea
- Regional security in East Asia
- International governance and non-proliferation
